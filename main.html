My Favorite Holiday Destination: Kyoto, Japan


Kyoto is a city on the island of Honshu in Japan, known for its classical Buddhist temples, as well as gardens, imperial palaces, Shinto shrines, and traditional wooden houses. It is a city that beautifully blends the past and present.

  1. Kinkaku-ji - The Golden Pavilion, a stunning Zen temple.
  2. Fushimi Inari Taisha - Famous for its thousands of vermilion torii gates.
  3. Arashiyama Bamboo Grove - A serene and picturesque bamboo forest.
  4. Kiyomizu-dera - A historic temple with breathtaking views.
  5. Nijo Castle - A UNESCO World Heritage Site with beautiful gardens.
  • Ramen - A popular noodle soup dish.
  • Sushi - Fresh fish served over vinegared rice.
  • Matcha - Traditional powdered green tea.
  • Yudofu - A simple yet delicious tofu hot pot.
  • Mochi - Sweet rice cakes with various fillings.
